Default Priority

Choose the default monitoring frequency for new products added via the extension.

ℹ️ Priority Levels Explained:
  • Low: Product prices checked once per day
  • Normal: Product prices checked every hour
  • High: Product prices checked every 15 minutes

You can change individual product priorities later in the PriceTracker web interface.
